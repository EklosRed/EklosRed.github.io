2025年伊朗国家黑白棋锦标赛 (2025 National Iranian Championship)

Written by Ali Talebi Zadeh

The 2025 edition of the National Iranian Othello Championship was successfully held on May 1st and 2nd, 2025 (equivalent to 10th and 11th Ordibehesht 1404 in the Iranian calendar) in Rafsanjan , a city in Kerman Province, Iran . The event took place at the Dopamin Game Hall , located within the Khoshgedeh Agah Cultural and Sports Complex .

2025年伊朗国家黑白棋锦标赛于2025年5月1日至2日（即伊朗历1404年Ordibehesht月10日至11日）在伊朗克尔曼省拉夫桑詹市成功举办。赛事在Khoshgedeh Agah文化体育综合中心内的Dopamin Game Hall举行。

A total of 23 players participated in the championship, including 12 players under the age of 18 , showcasing the growing interest and development of Othello among younger players in Iran. 本届锦标赛共有23名选手参赛，其中包括12名18岁以下选手，彰显了黑白棋运动在伊朗青少年中的蓬勃发展。比赛采用六轮瑞士制赛程，确保选手无论初始排名如何都能获得公平对决机会（具体对阵与排名可查阅蓝字赛事链接）

After the Swiss rounds, three players – Mohammad Zamanzadeh , Amin Pour Hamzeh Asl , and Mohsen Karamad – were tied for the fourth position. To resolve this, a special ranking pool was conducted between them. Following three additional matches, Mohammad Zamanzadeh emerged as the fourth semifinalist.

瑞士轮结束后，Mohammad Zamanzadeh、Amin Pour Hamzeh Asl和Mohsen Karamad三位选手在第四名争夺中形成平局。为此赛事特别举行了加赛环节，经过三场附加赛后，Mohammad Zamanzadeh成功晋级四强。

The semifinals were then played in a two-leg (home and away) format :

  • Danial Pour Hamzeh Asl (1st seed) faced Mohammad Zamanzadeh (4th seed)
  • Reza Shayesteh (2nd) played against Mehdi Taheri (3rd)

半决赛采用两回合制（主客场）：
• Danial Pour Hamzeh Asl（头号种子）对阵Mohammad Zamanzadeh（四号种子）
• Reza Shayesteh（二号种子）迎战Mehdi Taheri（三号种子）

After exciting semifinal rounds, Danial Pour Hamzeh Asl and Reza Shayesteh advanced to the Final . Two intense games were played, both won by Danial , crowning him as the national champion.

经过激烈角逐，Danial Pour Hamzeh Asl与Reza Shayesteh会师决赛。在最终对决中，Danial两战全胜问鼎冠军。

The event was officiated and supervised by Ali Talebizadeh-Sardari , who served as the head referee. The matches were also broadcast live via LiveOthello.com, enabling fans and players across the country to follow the action.

赛事由首席裁判Ali Talebizadeh-Sardari全程监督执裁，并通过LiveOthello.com平台进行实时直播。

The event was generously sponsored by Dopamin Game World , a prominent board game hub in Rafsanjan, which provided both the venue and logistical support.

本次锦标赛由拉夫桑詹知名桌游中心"Dopamin Game World"提供场地及后勤支持。
